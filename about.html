About Me

Some lessons that I've learned.

My name is Nicholas Maglioli. I was born and raised in Oshawa, Ontario, Canada. I love spending time with my family and talking to whoever I can.

Notably, I served two years as a volunteer representative in Winnipeg, Canada. There I developed important teaching and leadership skills that have helped my life. Many sales skills and techniques were involved, but most important was the quality of being altruistic. I'd love to share some of my experiences with you! Just ask.

I've worked as a laborer for Analytic Carpentry. My job was to manufacture and produce custom cabinetry. I learned how to build most of a kitchen in just a few weeks of starting! Aside from the carpentry skills, I learned the value of team-oriented hard work. Communication was the key to success and fulfillment.

Most recently, I worked as a sales representative for Vantage Marketing. I thought I was selling pest control, but I learned the value of interacting with other people and helping their needs feel validated and met. An important skill I will treasure!
